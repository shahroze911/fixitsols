An excellent scholarship can be beneficial if you have an undergraduate degree and want to continue your education in the UK. You can earn up to a £10,000 scholarship on tuition fees for a one-year-taught post-graduate program. The UK government’s GREAT Britain Campaign funds this scholarship with partner UK universities.

Chevening Scholarship

Another scholarship is offered by the UK Foreign, Commonwealth, and Development Office. This scholarship covers the full, fully funded UK scholarship for Pakistani students and the full tuition fee. Moreover, it also offers a monthly living allowance and return airfare of economy class. Besides, it also provides the cost of one visa application and a travel grant to participate in Chevening events in the UK. The student must show leadership qualities, an impressive academic background, experience, and a positive attitude.

Common Wealth Scholarship and Fellowship Plan

The UK’s CSC (Commonwealth Scholarship Commission) has funded over 30,000 Pakistani students since 1960. These scholarships are given to students with high potential to bring a positive change globally. If you have the potential to bring a change in the world, then you can also join this scholarship program. However, you must have a bachelor’s degree (2:1) to meet the requirements for the program.

Rhodes Scholarship

The Rhodes Scholarship is also a fully funded scholarship for Pakistani students in the UK. Rhodes Trust offers this program and covers student’s tuition fees, living costs, and travel costs. As a Pakistani student, you should have a bachelor’s degree to get that scholarship. The age limit is between 19 to 25 years. Again, this scholarship also demands academic excellence and the potential to change the world positively.

Gates Cambridge Scholarship

The Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation offers the Gates Cambridge Scholarship. This UK scholarship for Pakistani students covers the full tuition fee, living expenses, airfare, and many other benefits. Like the above scholarship programs, this program is also for PhD and Master’s programs. Pakistani students must have academic excellence and potential leadership qualities.

Scholarship Offered by Universities

Many universities in the UK offer fully-funded scholarships for Pakistani students. They convert different expenses from tuition fees to living costs. Here are some popular UK scholarships for Pakistani students provided by universities:

1. Oxford-Weidenfeld and Hoffmann Scholarships

    The Oxford-Weidenfeld and Hoffmann Scholarships cover Pakistani students’ tuition fees and living expenses. They help the students study a full-time master’s degree at Oxford University. In addition to the university fee, the scholarship covers other costs, including travelling expenses. Besides, the students can also develop professionally by attending seminars and networking events.

    2. University of Manchester Global Futures Scholarship

      The University of Manchester also offers scholarships for Pakistani students to study in the UK. Its Global Futures Scholarship is for those who want to pursue a postgraduate degree. As a fully funded scholarship program, it covers tuition fees and living expenses of the students. This scholarship is for students who have impressive academic histories and the potential to be leaders.

      3. University of Warwick Chancellor’s International Scholarship

        If you seek a master’s degree in the UK, this scholarship is for you. University of Warwick Chancellor’s International Scholarship is for students who are exceptional in their academics and possess leadership qualities. You can have up to £10,000 for one educational year.

        Private UK Scholarships for Pakistani Students

        Other than the above scholarships for Pakistani students abroad, some private scholarships are also available. These are not from the universities or institutes available in the UK but are locally funded. Here are some popular scholarships you can go for:

        1. Dawood Foundation Scholarship

          If you want to study at the University of Cambridge, the Dawood Foundation Scholarship is for you. TDF supports education in science, technology, and research areas. This organization offers you an annual living allowance of £15,000 other than tuition fees.

          2. British Council Charles Wallace Scholarship

            British Council Charles Wallace Scholarship is for Pakistani students who want to pursue a Master’s degree in the UK. It covers fees up to £18000 GBP, living expenditures up to £15000, and flight and research expenses up to £2000 and £4000, respectively. Moreover, Pakistani students also get training about the UK’s culture and how to live there.

            3. Commonwealth Shared Scholarship Program

              The Commonwealth Shared Scholarship is for students who want to pursue a postgraduate degree in the UK. Your country must be listed with the Commonwealth Scholarship Commission UK. The scholarship lasts 12 months and is for those who have the potential to bring about positive change. It includes more than 26 master’s and 30 PhD programs. This scholarship covers Pakistani students’ tuition fees and travel and living costs.
