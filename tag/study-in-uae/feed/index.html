Requirements of Student Visa for UAE

It would help if you had sponsorship from your university to get a student visa for UAE. However, you might need the help of education consultants in Islamabad. Similarly, they will help you get a visa for UAE and offer letters from the university. The application fee for student visas can be more than AED1000 plus VAT. Additionally, the total visa price for UAE is higher than AED3500. For a UAE visa, you will need to provide the following:

  1. A passport with a validity of 6 months or more!
  2. Passport size photographs
  3. Copies of passport and entry visa
  4. Proof of admission to the relevant institution
  5. Evidence of paid fees
  6. Proof of paid tuition fee
  7. Proof of paid visa fee
  8. Certificate or degree if applying for masters
