Accepted English Language Tests Other Than IELTS for UK Universities

UK universities without IELTS offer many other options for Pakistani students to pursue their education in the UK. There are alternative English proficiency tests for students so that they can be admitted easily. These tests also assess students’ English language skills, including reading, listening, writing, and speaking. The most common tests are:

  • TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)
  • PTE (Pearson Test of English)
  • Duolingo English Test
  • Oxford ELLT (English Language Level Test)
  • LanguageCert (Language Skills Assessment and Certification)
  • Cambridge English Exams (such as C1 Advanced & C2 Proficiency)
  • MOI (Medium of Instruction)

These tests assess the same skills as IELTS. However, their scoring system is a little different. Don’t be confident about applying for UK universities directly with any of these tests. The reason is that the test requirements are different for each university. It’s possible for one university to accept PTE while others go for Oxford ELLT. Therefore, it’s best to leave this task to your best education consultant.

Requirements of UK Universities Without IELTS for Admissions

The UK universities have certain requirements to give students admission without IELTS. Each university has its own criteria and selection procedure, so it’s best to do some research and process the details accordingly. Here are some requirements you will find while applying to a UK university:

  1. English language skills should be equal to CLB Level B2 (Upper-Intermediate) or Higher. CLB stands for Canadian Language Benchmarks.
  2. For MOI, the student should have 70% or above marks in English if the university needs an IELTS overall test score of 6.5 with individual bands no less than 5.5.
  3. Short courses in English and 60% in 10th-12th standard English are also acceptable.
  4. If you don’t have any English language test, the universities can offer a pre-sessional course in the language.
  5. A Bachelor’s degree in English can help a little, but it’s not compulsory.

FAQs

Do I need IELTS to Study in the UK?

Well, IELTS is not a must to study in the UK. However, proof of English proficiency is a must; that’s the reason why universities opt for different English test alternatives.

What are some major alternatives to IELTS?

TOEFL, PTE, Language Cert, Oxford ELLT, Duolingo, and Cambridge are some of the major alternatives to IELTS.

Can I study in the UK without taking any English tests?

Yes, you can study in the UK without any English test, but you still need to provide UK universities with proof of English proficiency. For this, the university should accept MOI (Medium of Instruction). However, there are very few universities in the UK that accept MOI from very few Pakistani universities.

Which UK universities accept students without IELTS?

Many UK universities accept students without IELTS, starting from the University of Bristol and Greenwich to Birmingham City University and London Metropolitan University.
