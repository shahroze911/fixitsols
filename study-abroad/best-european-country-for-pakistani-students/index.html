Why Study in Europe? A Gateway for Pakistani Students

The best countries in Europe for higher education offer the enviable mix of academic rigor and practical benefits. Universities in Europe for international students provide English-taught programs with significant scholarships and opportunities for a successful career. What’s more, if you study in Europe, you will have access to the Schengen Area and visit or travel to 27 other countries while going to school.

Moving from busy universities in Pakistan to a European university is a big shift from the day-to-day campus to a more edifying classroom environment. Once on campus, you’ll get exposure to international perspectives, advanced labs, and industry connections. And all of which add value with respect to the job market. According to recent statistics, more than 50,000 students from Pakistan are enrolled in universities in Europe, with some regions offering tuition fees as low as zero. But what makes Europe the best country for higher education?

Europe ticks off many of these boxes:

  • You can choose from a variety of the best universities in Europe that offer globally recognized degrees.
  • Many European countries rank among the top priorities for international students, offering English-taught programmes, engaging cultural environments, and solid reputations.
  • Some countries also have very low or zero tuition costs (or reasonable tuition fees) for international students, making them the cheapest countries to study in Europe.
  • There are work while studying and post-study work permit options for a few of the countries, making them good options for students who want to study and work.
  • Pakistani students care more about return on investment and visa success, which makes Europe a good option for students who want to earn while steadying their career pathway.

While there is no single best European country for Pakistani students, and no single option applies to every student, what matters is finding the optimal choice for you. So contact our best European consultants in Islamabad now.

Key Factors to Consider When Choosing the Best European Country for Pakistani Students

Before exploring country profiles, there are important factors to consider in choosing the best European country for Pakistani students. These are:

  1. Tuition fees + living costs, students can expect to budget between €800-€1,200/month to cover housing, food, transit, etc. in most urban cities.
  2. Admission requirements (i.e., 12 years of schooling, English testing, and local language).
  3. Visa requirements, preparation, and submission, bank statements (proving proof of expenses for 3 – 6 months, generally €10,000 – €12,000, COC, etc.).
  4. Option to work while they study, number of hours permitted working part-time, and ease of finding work.
  5. Length of time that a student can stay and work on a post-study work visa.
  6. Pathway to Permanent Residency (PR), or long-term stay
  7. Dependents allowed: Can you bring your spouse/children?
  8. Language of instruction: How many programs are offered in English?
  9. Reputation and ranking of universities (so you get value)
  10. Cultural, safety, and student-life environment for Pakistani students
  11. Support services and presence of Pakistani/Islamic community (for ease)
  12. Visa success rate from Pakistan and the presence of reliable visa consultants

More Helpful Insights to Study in Europe

For Pakistani candidates, the admissions success rate hovers around 70-95%, with thorough documentation playing a significant role. For admission to European universities, it is typically expected that you have completed 12 years of schooling (minimum marks of 60-70%), IELTS/TOEFL scores of 6.0 or higher (with some programmes now opting for Duolingo English Test), and a good statement of purpose. Work opportunities during studies are also favourable, with most countries allowing 20 hours of part-time employment during the academic term and full-time work during breaks. This helps the students with living expenses.

Post-study work visas allow extra time after study (typically up to 2 years) to look for a job. The PSW can lead to permanent residency (PR) in their country after 5 years of skilled employment. In addition, family members (spouses/children) can accompany them at the postgraduate level in many countries on a family visa (if the applicant is single), which adds appeal to the family application. Finally, look at the rankings of the best universities in UK, Ireland, and others and the cultural fit of the country (language and adjacent cultures).

After considering these points, here is a list of the best European countries for Pakistani students based on accessibility, costs, and potential outcomes for 2026-27.

Top 10 Best European Countries for Pakistani Students: Detailed Breakdown

We have compiled a list of the best European nations for international students, prioritizing ease for Pakistanis. Each country has a brief overview table for easy comparison. These countries make our list of the best countries for education thanks to their unique perks.

1. Germany

Germany remains the best European country for Pakistani students, thanks to its zero-tuition model and engineering-focused education system. Pakistani applicants are attracted to the organized programs and an abundance of jobs in tech and automotive. There are over 400 programs available in English, making it easy to feel integrated into the culture. However, if you learn a few basic German words, your life there will be much easier, and you will not have trouble meeting the employer’s requirements.

Germany’s appeal is rooted in its efficiency: you apply through Uni-Assist, you prove financial support, and you enjoy relatively student-friendly economic conditions. Graduates usually get jobs with Siemens or BMW and effortlessly switch to PR. For Pakistanis, this is the best country for international students to study and work, minimizing debt while maximizing opportunities.

2. United Kingdom

The United Kingdom remains a top choice for international students seeking to study at universities in Europe. The country combines a rich historical background and modern research. The residency pathways have been greatly expanded through the Graduate Route visa. You can find cities like London, which is alive and bustling, or the quietness of Scotland. This is a great place to experience diversity. Pakistani students especially enjoy the familiar English medium language and the large Desi Turkish communities. Thus, when choosing the best country for international students to study and work, consider the UK.

Most of the best universities in Europe promote careers in finance and tech. Move into your job easily, as so many Pakistanis do while working for the NHS or consulting firms. Besides, the bank statement and visa requirements for students are felxible. You can study in cheap UK unviersities and pay fee in installments. If prestige is your priority, the UK is probably the best European country for Pakistani students. The UK also considers the IHS Fee to be money shown on your bank statement. Additionally, you also need to do a TB test for a UK student visa.

3. Netherlands

The Netherlands is the best European country for Pakistani students. Its bike-friendly cities and the fact that English is the dominant language in the country, with 95% of the population speaking it fluently, make it the most reliable study option. As it focuses on design and sustainability, this destination is perfect for creative Pakistanis.

Affordable housing (€400- €700/month) and cafes along the canal make studying enjoyable. After graduation, go to Philips or Shell to get a job that leads to PR. This country is the best in Europe for combining study with fun.

4. France

France is the best European country for Pakistani students, and it is great to learn valuable skills in the art and culinary fields at affordable rates. This is the best European country for students in the humanities. While Paris attracts many, Lyon has quieter surroundings.

With Campus France, applications simplify. Eiffel Scholarships provide funding. Graduates prosper in luxury brands like LVMH. France is the best by a long shot for the cheapest country in Europe, with immersive experiences.

5. Italy

Italy has it all when it comes to the best European country for Pakistani students, thanks to its Renaissance roots and fashion programme. You can enjoy cheap public universities and a student life rich in pasta!

Fully funded scholarships cover 50-100% but you will need a very strong academic profile and a bank letter with proof of funds. After studying, design houses like Gucci hire immediately after. Italy offers the opportunity to study in Europe, with a beautiful element of timeliness.

6. Sweden

Sweden is the ideal European country for Pakistani students, as it prioritizes sustainability and equality. Except for the long winters, fika breaks, and inspired tech hubs throughout the country.

SI Scholarships focus on students from outside the EU. IKEA and Spotify invite graduates. Sweden is the best country for higher education in terms of safety and affordability.

7. Spain

Spain is the best European country for Pakistani students, given siestas and the people’s love of soccer. Business and tourism courses are abundant here.

There are plenty of MEC scholarships available. Inditex has also offered post-grad positions. The unique atmosphere of Spain makes universities in Europe particularly appealing to international students. 

8. Ireland

Ireland, the best European country for Pakistani students, is home to tech giants like Google in Dublin, making it easier for students to attend Irish universities.

Government scholarships are also very helpful. Pharmaceutical jobs also lead to Permanent Residency. Ireland is a winner for international students to study and work. The unemployment rate in Ireland was 4% in March 2025. It is consistently one of the richest countries in GDP per capita in the OECD and the EU-27. Ireland is one of the fastest-growing economies in the EU.

9. Cyprus

No one should be surprised that Cyprus is the best country in Europe for Pakistani students, with 99% guaranteed visa approval, beachside campuses, and EU membership that helps your degree go further.

Affordable and secure, Cyprus is a well-kept secret among the cheapest countries to study in Europe. It serves as a simple entrance into Europe.

10. Hungary

Hungary is the best country for Pakistani students, offering a favorable fee structure, internship opportunities, strong career prospects, and Schengen Visa eligibility. Besides, Budapest boasts an interesting history.

The attraction of thermal baths and lower costs make Hungary a perfect study destination. Post-study work options and 24 hours of part-time work options help you secure your future. Besides, you can get PR in only 5 years. To find the best European country for a career, Hungary is ideal.
